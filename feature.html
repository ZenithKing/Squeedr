Squeedr is a powerful multitasking tool anyone can use to create events, book appointments, and other services. Using its rich and exciting catalog of features, you can completely personalize your Squeedr experience.

Create events, list services, take appointments:
  • Your Squeedr page helps you list your services and accept appointments
  • Offer multiple services in one package and accept a single payment
  • Schedule resources and equipment for specific events
  • Limit number of appointments you will accept in day
  • Create group events and set number of participants
Advanced booking options:

With Squeedr, your clients can:

  • Schedule one-on-one meetings with you or a team member of their choice
  • Make an appointment with more than 1 team member at the same time
  • Set recurring appointments
  • Book multiple services at once
  • Sign up for webinars, classes, trainings, and other group events
Accomplish More As a Team:
  • Add users to your account and build your team
  • Create separate login for each team member
  • Personalized Squeedr page lists all your services and events
  • Set roles and privileges for individual team members
  • Automate assigning of meetings in a team
Multilocation:
  • Flexibility in creating appointments based on location and availability
  • Let invitees select the location for a meeting
  • Automatic time zone detection ensures appointments are displayed in your client’s local time
Integrations

Squeedr connects with all popular calendars including Google, iCal, and Office 365 to keep track of your availability and update your schedule automatically.
Seamlessly integrates with your favorite business apps to increase productivity and boost your marketing and sales efforts.
Enable Stripe and Paypal to collect payments from clients.
Let your clients book appointments directly from your website or Facebook page with customizable Squeedr embed options. Get your private API access key.

Marketing & Reports:
  • Google My Business lets you list your business online, making it discoverable on Google Search and Maps.
  • Convert Google searches into confirmed bookings with Reserve with Google. [Pop-up: This feature allows people using Google Searches, Google Maps, or Google Assistant to discover your business and make appointments directly from the search result page. (Video) ]
  • Add Google Tag Manager to optimize tracking and marketing
  • Create attractive offers, discounts, and gift cards
  • Track customer engagement and campaign conversions with Google Analytics
  • Get daily reports on staff and client activity
Notifications

Our real-time notifications feature keeps you and your clients updated on booking confirmations, cancellations, re-scheduled appointments, and sends reminders through e-mails and SMS alerts.

Customizations:

Different customization options to completely personalize the Squeedr experience. Set up your profile, Squeedr page, e-mail templates, SMS text, payments receipts, etc., to reflect your brand. Remove ‘Powered by Squeedr’ watermark from your Squeedr Page by subscribing to Pro account.

Access Squeedr on any device:

You can use Squeedr on your PC, laptop, or smartphone. Use Squeedr app to keep track of your appointments while you are on the move.

Security:

SSL certified, Squeedr guarantees the safety of your sensitive data and information.
