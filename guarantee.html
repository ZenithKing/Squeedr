Our guaranties

Squeedr guarantees the protection of the personal data of your clients

Squeedr uses two licensed health data hosts The health data of Squeedr users are stored in two hosts approved by the "Authorized Host of Health Data" (HADS) issued by the Ministry of Health and HIPPA

The freenlancers and their clients own their data, as it is the exclusive property of clients and the provider who use Squeedr. Squeedr teams simply do not have Access to it.

Zero advertising There is no advertising on Squeedr, neither for the provider, nor for health professionals.

Physical Infrastructure

Squedr application is hosted on Amazon Web Services. Squeedr physical infrastructure is hosted and managed within Amazon’s secure data centers and utilize the Amazon Web Service (AWS) technology. Amazon continually manages risk and undergoes recurring assessments to ensure compliance with industry standards. Amazon’s data center operations have been accredited under:

  • ISO 27001
  • SOC 1 and SOC 2/SSAE 16/ISAE 3402 (Previously SAS 70 Type II)
  • PCI Level 1
  • FISMA Moderate
  • Sarbanes-Oxley (SOX)
