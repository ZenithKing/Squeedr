What can you do with Squeedr

Let Squeedr manage your time & services
  • Create your Mobile Platform in minutes For you and your team
  • Have a One To One or Group meeting via; Web conf, Voice, Video
  • Schedule your meetings among your team membre automaticly or let your client choose the team membre of their choice
  • Have a Squeedr page to display the entire teams and services to facilitat your booking
